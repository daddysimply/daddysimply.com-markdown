Ah, family movie nights—there’s nothing quite like it\! I still remember heading to Blockbuster with my best friend, scanning the aisles for the best movies for tweens, and arguing over which one we’d rent.

Before social media and Netflix, VCRs and DVD players ruled the living room, and a good family movie was everything.

Now that my son is getting older and navigating elementary school, I’ve been on the hunt for great kids’ movies we can enjoy together. Below is a carefully chosen list of movies for tweens and teens from the U.S., South Korea, India, and Canada.

Grab some snacks and enjoy.

\---

\#\# Best Movies for Tweens & Teens

\#\#\# Kiki’s Delivery Service (1989)

A young witch starts her own delivery service in a seaside town and learns independence, friendship, and confidence.  

\*\*Runtime:\*\* 102 min | \*\*Rating:\*\* G

\#\#\# Short Circuit (1986)

A lightning strike brings a military robot to life, leading to adventure and heartwarming discoveries.  

\*\*Runtime:\*\* 98 min | \*\*Rating:\*\* PG

\#\#\# Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles: Mutant Mayhem (2023)

The turtles return with action, humor, and pizza-fueled fun.  

\*\*Runtime:\*\* 99 min | \*\*Rating:\*\* PG
